Currently, I work at Galileo Robotics Company, mainly focused on vision-based motion control and debugging of large industrial-grade robotic dogs.

## Experience
- **Galileo Robotics Company** (2025.07–Present)  
  Robotics Control Engineer  
  - Ontology-aware motion control for quadrupedal robots (including locomotion on various terrains, fall recovery, acrobatic maneuvers, etc.)
  - Vision-based motion control for quadrupedal robots, specifically designed for traversing discrete (recessed) terrains such as ditches and elevated platforms.
  - Has participated in patent filings, project development, and academic paper writing.

- **Beijing Jiaotong University** (2022.09–2025.06)  
  Graduate Student  
  - Researched model-based locomotion control and state estimation.
  - Implemented and validated control algorithms in simulation environments.
  - Built a custom small-scale quadruped robot from scratch using STM32 microcontroller, Raspberry Pi, and MX-106 servos.
